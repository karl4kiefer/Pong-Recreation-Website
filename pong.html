Pong

Created by Karl Kiefer

How to Play

  • Press and hold the arrow key up to move the right paddle up.
  • Press and hold the arrow key down to move the right paddle down.
  • Hit the ball with your paddle to bounce the ball towards the opponent.
  • To score, hit the ball to the other side and hope the opponent misses.
  • If you score, you will be awarded a point, and if the opponent scores, they will be awarded a point.
  • Follow the table and click the corresponding number on your keyboard to change your paddle color!
  • Note: You must select the window before using controls.
